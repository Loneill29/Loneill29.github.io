ABOUT ME

FULL STACK DEVELOPER

I'm a web developer living in Chicago, IL. After dabbling in graphic design early in my career, I found myself craving the interpersonal connection and fast pace that comes with the world of sales and recruiting. Five years later, a sales position at a major tech company opened my eyes to the huge role that technology plays in the success of every business and the experience of every consumer, regardless of the product being sold. My love for sales was rooted in helping businesses grow, but having a role in developing applications that could reach hundreds of people at once could multiply my impact. I enrolled in a full-stack development bootcamp and haven't looked back.


Web development allows me to design and create, positively impact the outreach of a company and the experience of its clients, and solve complex problems that challenge me daily. I'm passionate about helping businesses grow by increasing the impact of their web presence and making their brand come to life.
